# AI-BOS Product Vision

## Mission
To empower small and medium businesses with an AI-native Business Operating System (AI-BOS). AI-BOS transforms disjointed business operations into a single, cohesive, intelligent platform that doesn't just display data, but actively manages, analyzes, and executes business tasks safely under human supervision.

## Core Philosophy
1. **AI is the Operating System:** AI is not a chatbot bolted onto a dashboard. It is the connective tissue that understands the context of the entire business—inventory, finance, hr, operations—and anticipates needs.
2. **Safety and Verification First:** AI prepares actions; humans approve them. No destructive or high-risk actions are taken without explicit verification.
3. **Radical Transparency:** Every decision, every executed action, and every AI reasoning step is logged, explainable, and auditable.
4. **Modularity enables Scalability:** The system is broken into independent milestones (Inventory, Finance, CRM) that integrate seamlessly via a centralized core.
5. **Simplicity in UI, Complexity in AI:** The user experience should be simple, intuitive, and focused. The complexity of business logic is abstracted away by the AI Assistant.

## Milestones Roadmap
- **Milestone 1:** Foundation (Auth, Dashboard, Core Architecture)
- **Milestone 2:** AI CEO (Orchestrator, Chat Interface, Tools)
- **Milestone 3:** Inventory Intelligence
- **Milestone 4:** Finance Intelligence
- **Milestone 5:** Purchase Automation
